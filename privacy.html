Privacy Policy — SnowPro

Last updated: 2026-02-07

SnowPro collects and processes the following data:

  • Email address
  • Name and profile information
  • User-generated content (messages, profile details)
  • Uploaded documents (optional)

We use this data solely for:

  • Account authentication
  • Communication between users
  • Providing core application functionality

All data is stored securely using Firebase (Google Cloud).

SnowPro does not sell personal data. We do not share personal data with third parties, except as required to provide the service (for example, infrastructure providers such as Firebase) or if required by law.

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.
